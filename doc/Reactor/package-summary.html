Package Reactor

Class Summary

Component Component abstract class, used for all components that can fail.
Condenser Condenser that converts steam back to water.
CondenserPump Pump used to move coolant through the condenser.
Generator Generates power from an attached turbine.
MajorComponent MajorComponent abstract class, used for components that can carry water/steam and have pressure and temperature
Reactor The nuclear reactor, evaporates water to steam as part of the boiling water reactor model.
SteamValve Steam Valve system that controls the flow of steam between the reactor, turbine and condenser.
Turbine A turbine that converts steam passed over it to rotation, used in conjunction with a generator
WaterPump WaterPump which moves water from one MajorComponent to another
